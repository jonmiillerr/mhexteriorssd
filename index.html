About M&H Exteriors

M&H Exteriors is a small exterior finishing business that takes pride in detail work. My focus is on the parts of the home that people notice up close: around your windows and doors, along the roofline, and anywhere clean metal work makes a difference.

I care about straight lines, clean cuts, and solid installs. The goal is simple: exteriors that not only look good from the street, but also hold up over time.

What I Do

I don’t try to do everything. I stick to what I’m good at and do it right the first time.

• Window & door coverings: metal trim and wraps that protect framing and give your windows and doors a finished, professional look.

• Metal soffit: clean, vented soffit systems that help your home breathe while keeping the underside of your overhangs sharp and tidy.

• Fascia: durable metal fascia that lines up tight and straight along the roof edge.

• Break work: custom metal pieces bent to fit the job, from trims and flashings to small details that finish off a project the right way.
